# Step 10: Reconnect

## Objective

Reconnect all cables and connectors after display installation.

## Tools Required

- Plastic spudger
- Tweezers

## Connectors to Reconnect

### Battery Connector (J1)
1. Align battery connector
2. Push down firmly
3. Verify click

### Other Flex Cables
- Front sensor connector (J4)
- Rear camera connector (J5)
- Button flex connector (J6)

## Instructions

### 1. Reconnect Battery

1. Locate J1 battery connector
2. Align carefully
3. Push down until click
4. Verify secure

### 2. Reconnect Other Cables

1. Check each connector
2. Align properly
3. Lock in place
4. Test connection

### 3. Replace Shield

1. Position shield correctly
2. Press until clips engage
3. Replace shield screws

## Verification

- [ ] Battery connected
- [ ] Display connected
- [ ] Touch connected
- [ ] All cables secure
- [ ] Shield installed

## Notes

- Work carefully with connectors
- Ensure proper alignment
- Don't force connections
- Check each one
