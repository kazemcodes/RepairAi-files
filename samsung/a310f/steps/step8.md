# Step 8: Remove Display

## Objective

Carefully remove the display assembly from the device.

## Tools Required

- Heat gun or iOpener
- Plastic opening tools
- Suction cup
- Guitar pick or thin plastic

## Instructions

### 1. Apply Heat

1. Heat edges of display to soften adhesive
2. Focus on top and bottom edges
3. Temperature: ~60-70°C (140-160°F)
4. Heat for 1-2 minutes

### 2. Lift Display

1. Insert suction cup near top edge
2. Pull gently to create gap
3. Insert guitar pick or plastic tool
4. Work around edges slowly

### 3. Release Adhesive

1. Slide tool along edges
2. Cut adhesive strip
3. Work opposite corner
4. Continue until free

### 4. Remove Display

1. Ensure all connectors disconnected
2. Lift display assembly away
3. Set aside on soft surface

## Warnings

- Don't force the display
- Go slowly around edges
- Watch for hidden cables
- Don't puncture battery
