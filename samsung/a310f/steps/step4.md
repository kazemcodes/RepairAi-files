# Step 4: Remove Screws

## Objective

Remove all securing screws to access internal components.

## Tools Required

- Phillips #00 screwdriver
- Phillips #000 screwdriver
- Magnetic screw mat

## Screws to Remove

### Back Cover Screws (Already Removed)
- 4x Phillips #00 screws

### Main Board Shield Screws
| Screw | Type | Location | Quantity |
|-------|------|----------|----------|
| S1 | Phillips #00 | Shield corners | 3 |
| S2 | Phillips #000 | Display connector bracket | 2 |

### Battery Connector Screw
- 1x Phillips #00 screw (if present)

## Instructions

1. **Locate all screws** - Refer to [screws.md](../screws.md)
2. **Use correct screwdriver** - #00 for most, #000 for small screws
3. **Remove slowly** - Don't strip screws
4. **Organize** - Place on magnetic mat by location
5. **Label** - Use labels or take photos

## Screw Organization

Create sections on your magnetic mat:
- Back cover screws
- Shield screws
- Connector screws
- Other screws

## Notes

- Don't over-tighten or loosen
- Keep track of different sizes
- Some screws may have washers
- Check for hidden screws
