# Step 7: Disconnect Display

## Objective

Disconnect the display and touch screen connectors from the board.

## Tools Required

- Plastic spudger
- Tweezers

## Connector Locations

Refer to [board.md](../board.md):

| Connector | Type | Location | Purpose |
|-----------|------|----------|---------|
| J2 | 40-pin ZIF | Top center | Display |
| J3 | 6-pin ZIF | Top left | Touch panel |

## Instructions

### 1. Unlock Display Connector (J2)

1. Locate 40-pin display connector (J2)
2. Flip up the locking tab gently
3. Pull out the display flex cable

### 2. Unlock Touch Connector (J3)

1. Locate 6-pin touch connector (J3)
2. Flip up the small locking tab
3. Pull out the touch flex cable

## Notes

- Pull on connector, not cable
- Use plastic tools only
- Don't touch gold contacts
- Keep connectors clean
