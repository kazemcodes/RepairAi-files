# Step 1: Preparation

## Objective

Gather all required tools and prepare your workspace for the repair.

## Tools Required

- Phillips #00 screwdriver
- Phillips #000 screwdriver
- Plastic opening tools (set)
- Suction cup
- Tweezers (non-magnetic)
- Heat gun or iOpener
- Multimeter
- Antistatic mat
- Wrist strap
- Magnetic screw mat
- Clean workspace

## Workspace Setup

1. **Clean the area**: Remove all clutter from your work surface
2. ** ESD protection**: Place antistatic mat and connect wrist strap
3. **Lighting**: Ensure good lighting for detailed work
4. **Organization**: Set up containers for screws and small parts

## Device Preparation

1. **Backup data**: If possible, backup all device data
2. **Power down**: Ensure device is powered off completely
3. **Remove SIM**: Eject SIM tray and remove SIM card
4. **Remove SD**: Remove SD card if present

## Pre-Repair Checklist

- [ ] All tools gathered
- [ ] Workspace prepared
- [ ] ESD protection in place
- [ ] Device backed up
- [ ] Device powered off
- [ ] SIM/SD cards removed
- [ ] Have replacement parts ready

## Notes

- Take photos before disassembly for reference
- Work on a white mat to see small screws
- Keep screws organized by removal order
- Label connectors and cables
