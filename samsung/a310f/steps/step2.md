# Step 2: Power Off

## Objective

Safely power down the device before starting repairs.

## Instructions

1. **Press and hold** the power button
2. **Select "Power Off"** from the menu
3. **Wait** for device to fully shut down
4. **Verify**: Screen should be completely black

## For Unresponsive Devices

If the device is frozen or unresponsive:

1. **Press and hold** power button for 10 seconds
2. Device should force power off
3. If still on, wait for battery to drain completely
4. Remove battery if accessible (see Step 3)

## Safety Precautions

- Never work on device while powered on
- Remove battery as soon as possible
- Avoid static electricity when device is on
- Don't touch components while powered

## Verification

- [ ] Screen is completely off
- [ ] No LED indicators blinking
- [ ] Device is cool to touch
- [ ] No sounds or vibrations
