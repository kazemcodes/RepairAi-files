### Tools Required
- Phillips #00 screwdriver
- Phillips #000 screwdriver
- Plastic opening tools
- Suction cup
- Tweezers

- Battery adhesive strips

### Notes
- Use only OEM or high-quality third-party parts
- Verify part compatibility before ordering
- Keep track of small components
- Document screw locations
